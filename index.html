About Us

Park Smart is a smart parking solution company who strongly believe to make life easier by developing smart technology services in order to create new urban mobility models for smart cities.

At Park Smart we have the vision of working to overcome this challenge by contribute with our technology software to build a more efficient city-life, letting citizen saving precious time avoiding stressful situations, reducing pollution and traffic congestion in order to create positive effects in terms of quality of life on the whole community.


What We Do?

Computer Vision & Deep Learning

Using network cameras as multisensor, our algorithms are able to analyze the scenes to extract the informations that will be sent to our back-end in Cloud.

Distributed Computing On The Edge

To drastically reduce bandwidth occupancy all elaborations are made on the AISee Box, our IoT embedded device able to run our algorithms on it. It is possbile to put AISee Box on the streets in a cabinet or in Network Operations Center.

Privacy Protected

We destroy all frames captured by cameras after the automatic elaboration operated by computer vision algorithms inside See Box.

Easy Integration

Our back-end provide JSON API that can be use to receive real time data of slot occupancy.

Our Products?

Save Time - Save Nerves

Drivers globally spend an average of nearly 15 minutes per trip in pursuit of a parking space. With Park Smart App, the Users will save precious time avoiding stressful situations.

Maximise Revenue

Parking management companies lose up to 50% of their revenue. Thanks to Park Smart software based technology, Parking Management Companies will be able to increase their revenue by up to 30%, thanks to the possibility to control in real time the entire available parking spaces.
